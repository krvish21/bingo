🎯 How to Play

📋 Game Rules

  • Complete tasks from your daily work activities
  • Click on a square when you accomplish that task
  • Get 5 squares in a row (horizontal, vertical, or diagonal) to win
  • The center square is a FREE SPACE - automatically marked
  • Multiple bingos can be achieved on the same board

🏆 Winning Types

  • Horizontal: Complete any full row
  • Vertical: Complete any full column
  • Diagonal: Complete either diagonal line
  • Four Corners: Complete all four corner squares

🎮 Game Features

  • Your progress is automatically saved
  • View all winners in the winners panel
  • Reset your board anytime to start fresh
  • Celebrate your achievements with confetti!

💡 Tips

  • Focus on tasks you do regularly for easy wins
  • Keep track of your daily activities
  • Try to complete multiple bingo patterns
  • Share your wins with your team!
